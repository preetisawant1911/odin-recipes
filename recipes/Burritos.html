Burrito recipe

Ingredients

  • Large flour tortillas
  • Cooked rice (Mexican or plain)
  • Black beans (or pinto beans)
  • Grilled chicken (or beef/pork as alternative protein)
  • Shredded cheese (cheddar/Monterey Jack)
  • Salsa (any spice level)
  • Guacamole (or sliced avocado)
  • Sour cream (or Mexican crema)
  • Cilantro (fresh garnish)

Procedure

  • Prepare the filling: Warm the black beans in a pan (season with a pinch of salt, cumin, or chili powder if you like).Dice and sauté onions until golden.Cook or reheat grilled chicken, then cut into bite-size pieces.
  • Warm the tortilla:Heat each tortilla on a flat pan/tawa for 20–30 seconds per side so it stays soft and pliable.
  • Layer the ingredients: Place tortilla flat.Add a scoop of rice in the center.Top with beans.Add chicken pieces.Sprinkle shredded cheese..
  • Add fresh toppings:Spoon some sautéed onions.Add a small dollop of guacamole.Put salsa on top.Add a little sour cream.Sprinkle fresh cilantro.
  • Roll the burritos:Fold the sides inward, then roll from the bottom up tightly.If needed, warm it again on a pan seam-side down for 1–2 minutes so it seals and cheese melts..
